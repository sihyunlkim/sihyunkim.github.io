About the Film

Have you ever been lost in the Arts Center?

The NYUAD Arts Center is like a maze. Everyone who first came to the NYUAD Arts Center panicked at least once, wandering through the labyrinth of corridors and studios. This film captures that moment of being lot in the Arts center. It is not just a story of getting lost- it's about finding something unexpected along the way.
