About Me

Hi! My name is Sihyun Kim, and I’m currently a sophomore at New York University Abu Dhabi, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Interactive Media. My academic journey is fueled by a deep interest in technology, design, and problem-solving, and I aspire to become a full-stack developer capable of creating innovative digital solutions that make a tangible impact.

Growing up across South Korea, the Philippines, and Canada, I was immersed in diverse cultures and perspectives, which instilled in me a global mindset and a passion for learning. This multicultural upbringing has not only shaped my worldview but also sparked my curiosity about how technology can bridge cultural and social gaps.

In the future, I hope to combine my technical expertise in computer science with my creative interests in interactive media to develop innovative solutions that solve real-world challenges while engaging and inspiring users worldwide.
